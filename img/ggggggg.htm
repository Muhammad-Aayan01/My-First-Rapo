    Care

    To keep your AURAWṜ watch looking sharp and working perfectly, follow these simple care tips:

    • Avoid direct contact with water — our watches are water-resistant, not waterproof.
    • Keep away from perfumes, oils, and chemicals that may affect the finish.
    • Use a soft cloth to gently wipe the watch face and strap after wearing.
    • Store in a dry, cool place when not in use — ideally in the original box or a soft pouch.
    • Avoid strong magnetic fields to protect the movement mechanism.

    With a little care, your AURAWṜ watch will stay timeless — just like your aura.
